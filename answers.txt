Q1: Why might be consider using git?

Answer: Using Git helps us have efficient version control. It allows you to track changes and collaborate easily with peers on coding projects. It also gives us a good way to manage different versions of files. This way, if we wanted to revert to old stages, it works!

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an example and how it might help.

Answer: Data science can be applied to the healthcare field to analyze patient data. 
For example, the field of predictive analytics can predict disease outbreaks by 
examining historical health records and environmental factors and analyzing them.
